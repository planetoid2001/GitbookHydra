# Hydra Bridge

## What is Hydra Chain's MultiBridge?

Hydra chain native multi-chain bridge is a cross-chain bridge that allows exchange of crypto assets between Ethereum, Hydra, BNB Chain and Polygon blockchains.&#x20;

Users holding assets on Ethereum or the other mentioned chains can exchange them to corresponding assets on Hydra (1:1). Hydra MultiBridge also allows redemption of the exchanged assets back to the user's Ethereum or the other mentioned chains account at any time.

### How to use the MultiBridge

1. Visit <https://multibridge.hydrachain.org/>  on your Laptop or the in-wallet browser of your preferred wallet, like [Rabby](https://rabby.io/) or [MetaMask](https://metamask.io/en-GB/download).

2. Connect your Wallet to the multibridge and ensure that you have selected the blockchain that you want to bridge funds from, on the top right side of the page<br>

   <figure><img src="https://2559938628-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MLNXvXNFPLXIrp6TqdS%2Fuploads%2FNarqXSaG5B5musYfQK7x%2FConnect_wallet.PNG?alt=media&#x26;token=64b39a02-0933-4691-b38b-7265c495b4ba" alt=""><figcaption><p>Connect Wallet and choose blockchain</p></figcaption></figure>

3. Select the Origin chain as either Ethereum or polygon or BNB Chain (depending on where you are bridging your funds from) and **HydraGon** as the destination chain. Your wallet's EVM address that is connected will automatically populate **(Insert Pic Below)**<br>

   <figure><img src="https://2559938628-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MLNXvXNFPLXIrp6TqdS%2Fuploads%2FBgW2MWj6Il1mq3e20IRA%2F2select_chain2.PNG?alt=media&#x26;token=e4bb689c-a135-42b8-b30b-efeffe91b8df" alt=""><figcaption><p>Choose Origin and Destination Chains</p></figcaption></figure>

4. Select the token you wish to bridge to HydraGon from the dropdown and input the amount in the adjoining box.<br>

   <figure><img src="https://2559938628-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MLNXvXNFPLXIrp6TqdS%2Fuploads%2FbO0Zo6mB5TIp962SyQ72%2F3select_token.PNG?alt=media&#x26;token=89998c9e-f3a6-4b3c-abd8-98e3ef8e0c6a" alt=""><figcaption><p>Select Token, Enter amount, Enter Destination Address &#x26; Click on Approve</p></figcaption></figure>

5. In the "Destination wallet address" box, Input your MetaMask / Rabby wallet address (this will be the destination of the bridged funds and will be pre-populated with the address you are logged in with). Please ensure that you are entering the correct address that you have access to. It can be the same EVM address that you use on Ethereum, Polygon or BNB Chain.<br>

   <figure><img src="https://2559938628-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MLNXvXNFPLXIrp6TqdS%2Fuploads%2FGrYofgbgEOXknb01JrTI%2F4Approve.PNG?alt=media&#x26;token=ea505fd6-fd0d-4c7c-ae8f-7414efd923b1" alt=""><figcaption><p>Confirm the transaction in your Wallet</p></figcaption></figure>

6. Click On Approve and confirm the transaction in your wallet. This is a one time transaction approving the use of the token from your wallet.<br>

   <figure><img src="https://2559938628-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MLNXvXNFPLXIrp6TqdS%2Fuploads%2FeuycBLa3ABelQM7OUwZ9%2F6transfer.PNG?alt=media&#x26;token=638c3ad3-1c3e-4c9c-b8c7-fdff80015a6f" alt=""><figcaption><p>Click on Transfer after the Approve transaction is processed</p></figcaption></figure>

7. Click on Transfer and confirm the transaction in your wallet. Remember you need to first "Approve" (Step 6 above) and then "Transfer", therefore there are 2 transactions if bridging for the first time for each token.<br>

   <figure><img src="https://2559938628-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MLNXvXNFPLXIrp6TqdS%2Fuploads%2FCrr38WU6AbHEDjvb739B%2F7confirm_transfer.PNG?alt=media&#x26;token=d256dff1-3832-4aef-8d02-a55f18f52369" alt=""><figcaption><p>Confirm this transaction as well in your Wallet</p></figcaption></figure>

8. After confirming the transaction, wait for 10 to 15 minutes and your funds will arrive in your destination wallet on HydraGon. <br>

   <figure><img src="https://2559938628-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MLNXvXNFPLXIrp6TqdS%2Fuploads%2F6OkpMUsvIThybEHhAzO3%2F8transfer_submitted.PNG?alt=media&#x26;token=9fe901bd-864a-49e1-ab54-28ea854a3d88" alt=""><figcaption><p>Transfer confirmed, Wait for 10 - 15 Minutes</p></figcaption></figure>

9. You can view the bridging progress on the History tab of the MultiBridge. Once it shows as Completed, the assets will have arrived in your wallet on HydraGon. On occasion, it may take longer (up-to 2 hours). Please ensure that you have added the token contract to your wallet to be able to view the token balance. You can acquire the token contract from the explorer <https://skynet.hydrachain.org/tokens><br>

   <figure><img src="https://2559938628-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MLNXvXNFPLXIrp6TqdS%2Fuploads%2FafKrm040hxYGlCP7QIsx%2F9check_status_history.PNG?alt=media&#x26;token=c74ae3a1-e7ed-4084-88d3-3fb825b84007" alt=""><figcaption><p>View bridging progress on History tab </p></figcaption></figure>

That's it! You can now use your bridged assets to participate in various De-Fi activities like Liquidity Provision, Trading ecosystem assets etc. [HydraDEX](https://hydradex.org/) also has some attractive [Liquidity Mining incentives](https://docs.hydrachain.org/hydradex/liquidity-mining) provided to the Liquidity Providers as a reward for supplying liquidity.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Please note: Bridged assets can only be used in the Hydra ecosystem and no centralized exchange supports deposit of the bridged assets at this time.**
{% endhint %}

# Staking on HydraGon

## Staking Overview

Staking on Hydra Chain involves delegating HYDRA coins in the network to support network operations like securing the blockchain and validating transactions. In return, participants receive rewards which are influenced by several dynamic factors such as loyalty (vesting period), validator performance and market conditions.

* **Vesting Period**: The number of weeks you lock your HYDRA for.
* **Macro Factor**: Adjusts rewards based on HYDRA's market trends.
* **RSI Bonus**: Provides additional rewards based on relative market strength.

### Claiming Rewards

1. **Unvested Positions**
   1. Rewards accumulate every Epoch (500 Blocks or \~ 4 to 5 minutes)
   2. Unvested delegated positions can claim the accumulated rewards at any time, by clicking on the orange "Claim Rewards" button to the right under "Action" <br>

      <figure><img src="https://2559938628-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MLNXvXNFPLXIrp6TqdS%2Fuploads%2Fb7QFSrTBEQFO5MOeGQMT%2F1Claimunvested.PNG?alt=media&#x26;token=18525e31-edea-4e86-ba62-ec7680f82c2b" alt=""><figcaption><p>Claim Rewards</p></figcaption></figure>
2. **Vested Delegated Positions**
   1. Rewards are "Generated" as soon as you begin your vested delegated position, however cannot be claimed until after the vesting period ends.<br>

      <figure><img src="https://2559938628-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2F-MLNXvXNFPLXIrp6TqdS%2Fuploads%2FJrwUQEZX71mcdgNN0e4a%2F1Generated%20Rewards.PNG?alt=media&#x26;token=32105705-c279-4648-b6e0-fd24dd528dc1" alt=""><figcaption><p>Generated Rewards</p></figcaption></figure>
   2. The rewards can be claimed gradually after the respective vesting period has elapsed as and when they "mature". Rewards are claimable in batches based on their individual maturity as shown the the "Rewards Maturity" Column. \
      \
      For Example: &#x20;
      1. In a **12-week vesting period**, the rewards are released in sequence:
         * Week 1 rewards → Claimable after Week 13.
         * Week 2 rewards → Claimable after Week 14, and so on.
      2. In a **52-week vesting period**, the rewards are released in sequence:
         * Week 1 rewards → Claimable after Week 52.
         * Week 52 rewards → Claimable after Week 102.
      3. For instance: If the Rewards Maturity column shows 15% Matured, this means that 15% of your rewards have matured and are claimable.<br>

**Important Notes**

* Each epoch’s reward is tied to validator performance. Validators who miss attestations or underperform will reduce the rewards available to their delegators​​.
* If you do not undelegate your Hydra after the vesting period ends, you will continue to earn the "Unvested APR" until you undelegate; and these rewards are claimable anytime.

## ⚡ **Important to Know**

* **Cooldown Period:** After Undelegating any type of staked position, there’s a <mark style="color:yellow;">7-day cooldown</mark> before the coins can be withdrawn to your wallet, which safeguards against panic-induced network instability​.
* **Penalties for early undelegation:** If you undelegate before your vesting period is over, you will face a <mark style="color:yellow;">**penalty (0.5% per remaining week)**</mark>, and lose any accumulated rewards.

## How does it all work

### Vesting and Its Dynamics

When staking with vesting, users lock their funds for a chosen duration in exchange for loyalty bonuses on the APR. Rewards for vested positions grow exponentially with the length of the lockup

1. **Reward Calculation**:
   * Base APR: Default rate is **5%**.
   * Vesting Bonus:  $$Bonus APR=0.15×(vesting weeks)^{1.5}$$
   * Example:
     * A **12-week vesting period** gives an additional bonus of approximately <mark style="color:orange;">**6.23% APR**</mark>.
     * A **52-week vesting period** adds a significant bonus of <mark style="color:green;">**21.78% APR**</mark>**.**
2. **Premature Undelegation Penalty**:
   * Penalty: **0.5% of the staked amount per remaining week** of the lockup.
   * All unclaimed rewards are burned if undelegated prematurely.
   * Example:
     * A user prematurely cancels a 52-week lockup with 10 weeks remaining: **Penalty = 10 weeks × 0.5% =&#x20;**<mark style="color:red;">**5% of the stake**</mark>​​ and all accumulated rewards of the past 42 weeks are **burned**

### The Macro Factor

The protocol automatically optimizes the staking APR based on the market cycle in real time, making it self-aware. It Acts as a global multiplier to the Base APR and Vesting Bonus. Boosts the APR during bullish periods and lowers it at bearish times. The goal of this feature is to introduce a dynamic macro factor that protects the network from **unnecessary inflation** during bearish market phases.

* The Staking page at <https://stake.hydrachain.org/> displays the <mark style="color:green;">**live**</mark> Macro Factor.

### The RSI Factor

The **RSI Bonus** (Relative Strength Index Bonus) is a short term & limited time Bonus incentive on new vested staking positions based on Hydra’s RSI levels  (up to 70%).

* Here is how it works:
  * If RSI is 30-35 <mark style="color:green;">**(Oversold)**</mark> Add <mark style="color:purple;">**+15% Bonus**</mark>
  * If RSI is 20-30 <mark style="color:orange;">**(Fear)**</mark> Add <mark style="color:purple;">**+25% Bonus**</mark>
  * If RSI is < 20 <mark style="color:red;">**(Extreme Fear)**</mark> Add <mark style="color:purple;">**+70% Bonus**</mark>
* Note: The above bonuses are not direct APR additions. Instead they are final multipliers on whatever APR the position would be yielding under normal conditions. E.g. a 10% default APR with a 70% bonus would amount to a 17% APR.

The combination of the Macro Factor and RSI Bonus ensures that rewards reflect both market trends and investor behavior
